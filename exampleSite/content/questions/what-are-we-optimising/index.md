A team can improve the speed, precision, and reliability of a process while
making the underlying situation worse.

Before choosing a measure, ask what disappears when the measure rises. Ask who
must absorb the exceptions. Ask whether the desired result can be observed
without reducing it to the easiest available number.

The premise is part of the system. It should be open to revision.
