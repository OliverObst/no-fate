**Demonstration notice:** All people, organisations, projects, publications, and
events shown here are fictional. The propositions and historical claims are
invented as well. This material exists only to demonstrate No Fate and should
be replaced or removed in a downstream site.

The Unfixed Record is a fictional publication about how arguments, tools, and
archives shape one another. Its deliberately varied fixtures test long-form
writing, questions, field notes, projects, and formal records without assuming
a real subject area or institution.
