The negative book records castings that never reached the polishing bench.
Each photograph carries a fault code, a short diagnosis, and an increasingly
elaborate metaphor for the noise made when the mould was opened.

## A useful catalogue of failure

Successive entries make recurring cracks easy to compare. The book was not a
memorial to mistakes. It was a tool for noticing when yesterday's mistake had
put on a different hat.
